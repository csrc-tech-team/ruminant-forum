4th Ruminant Forum (Hybrid)

Theme: “Game Changers in the Ruminant Industry”

November 21 – 22, 2024

The 4th Ruminant Forum brings together industry experts, researchers, and enthusiasts to discuss the latest innovations and game-changing developments in the ruminant industry. Join us for a two-day hybrid event at the University of the Philippines Los Baños to explore new prospects, technologies, and strategies shaping the future of ruminant farming.
